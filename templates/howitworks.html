How It Works

"Find Me a Whiskey" uses a dataset with reviews of over 2,000 whiskeys. The code uses natural language processing (NLP) to extract the flavours.

We use the most common flavours and tag the whiskeys with them based on their description.

As whiskeys can be described with so many different flavours, we use a word association technique to find synonyms, using Word2Vec.

Word2Vec

Word2vec is a technique for NLP. It uses a neural network model to learn word associations from a large corpus of text. Once trained, such a model can detect synonymous words.

After training with our descriptions plus reviews scraped from other websites, we have our synonyms

For example, one of our flavours is the word peat. Our code finds the following synonyms
